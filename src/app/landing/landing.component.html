About Us

Russel Street Medical opened in 2020, and is located in Melbourne's CBD at: 427 Swanston Street Melbourne 3000 - just opposite of RMIT University Building 10 - and within walking distance of Melbourne Central Station.

We strive to help all of our patients with a focus on preventative healthcare, a view to managinc chronic health conditions with a holistic approach, and with access to a wide range of specialist care providers when needed.

RMIT students and staff receive discounts through our partnership programs.
